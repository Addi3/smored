### What does this add / do?

- This takes inspiration from Outerwild's Marshmallow mechanics by adding the ability to cook them and much more...

### Will this mod be ported to forge or neoforge?

- Nope. I just don't have the time to maintain multiple mod versions. You can look at the `DEV TALK` page [here](https://addi3.github.io/) for a detailed answer.

### How do i get access to betas?

-   You can access betas via my [Ko-fi page](https://ko-fi.com/addi3dabaddie)!

### Can you add... / How do you use...?

- You can suggest stuff on the github issues page or on the my official [discord server](https://discord.gg/5JDKuzarcS)! I will also answer any questions on either site!
